## STEP 1 — 原则1: 能力圈 (Capability Circle)

### Three-test gate for jsonwzy:

#### Test ①: 5-sentence business description
Tesla is **four businesses bundled into one ticker**:
1. **Autos** (~80% of revenue): Premium EV maker; ~358K Q1 2026 deliveries (+6% YoY); ~14-19% gross margin; losing ground to BYD/Xiaomi/Foxconn in China; the cash engine.
2. **Energy** (~10%): Megapack + Powerwall + Solar; high-growth but lumpy (Q1 deployments fell 38% sequentially to 8.8 GWh).
3. **FSD/Robotaxi** (~1% revenue today, **>80% of market-cap thesis**): ~1.28M FSD subscribers, $546M ARR, ~14% take rate; Austin/Dallas/Houston unsupervised robotaxi service with ~44 vehicles vs Waymo's 3,000 / 500K weekly rides.
4. **Optimus** (humanoid robot, **pre-revenue, the moonshot**): production "later this year" per Musk Q1 call, but he explicitly said "impossible to predict" production rate.

**Verdict on Test ①:** Pass on description, **fail on synthesis** — these four businesses have wildly different moats, unit economics, and risks. Calling TSLA "one stock" is itself a category error.

#### Test ②: Predict downside floor — be HONEST
Per CLAUDE.md: **「懂」 = 能独立、可证伪地预判未来 (壁垒会不会变 / 增长靠什么 / 什么会打破它)。**

| **Thesis-bear (auto erodes vs BYD + capex burn + Musk pay dilution -12%, but TSLA stays as "premium EV-AI hybrid")** | **~$220-260** | -37% to -47% from $412 |

⚠️ **CLAUDE.md warning triggered:** "永远画得出 2x 但下限是 0/1 的标的 → 只能小仓"。 TSLA fits this exactly.
