## 1. 原则1 — 能力圈（资格） — **FAIL**

### 1.1 5 句话讲清生意 — partially yes, structurally no

Best honest 5-sentence summary I can write today:
1. **Taobao/Tmall Group (TTG)** is China's #1 GMV e-commerce marketplace; monetization is CMR (ad+commission take rate × GMV), with CMR re-accelerated to +8% YoY LFL in Q4 FY26 after multi-year stagnation.
2. **Cloud Intelligence Group (CIG)** is China's #1 IaaS+PaaS (~36% domestic share), now pivoting to MaaS via Qwen open-source models (1B+ HuggingFace downloads) and Bailian platform; AI products = 30% of external cloud revenue, growing triple digits 11 quarters running.
3. **Quick commerce** (Taobao Instant Commerce / Ele.me) is a multi-billion-RMB cash burn fighting Meituan/JD for a $126B-by-2029 TAM, 43% volume share, targeting unit-economics positive by end-FY27.
4. **International Digital Commerce (AIDC)** (AliExpress, Lazada, Trendyol) grew rapidly but EBITA still negative; **Cainiao** logistics integrated post-Sun Art/Intime divestitures.
5. Capital allocation = redirect 3-yr ~RMB 380B (~$53B) and likely more into AI capex (T-Head GPUs, 92 AZ across 29 regions), cut buybacks ~10x to fund it, maintain dividend.

**Where it fails the test:** I cannot **independently predict** moat evolution for any single line of business with falsifiable variables in 2-3 years:
- **Cloud:** Qwen vs DeepSeek/Doubao/Kimi/ByteDance Volcano — winner depends on PRC chip supply (T-Head Zhenwu vs SMIC vs HBM availability) + enterprise procurement decisions made opaque by SOE preference and state-directed compute allocation. I do not have a working model for "who wins China enterprise AI in 2 years."
- **CMR:** Take-rate stabilization vs Pinduoduo/Douyin live commerce — depends on whether merchant rebates roll off, which is a policy/competitive question, not a business-fundamentals question I can score.
- **Quick commerce:** Whether RMB 10B+/yr incentive burn converts to durable share or evaporates when Meituan responds — pure red-queen race, no defensible base case for floor.

→ "懂的本质 = 能对未来做独立可证伪预判" → I can describe what's happening (good) but I cannot predict who wins which layer in 2-3 years (圈外).

### 1.2 Bear floor — political tail is non-decomposable

Standard SOTP bear case (analyst common):
- Cloud at 6x EV/Sales (~$5.3B AI ARR + ~$15B traditional cloud ≈ $20B → $120B)
- TTG at 6x EBITDA (~$25B EBITDA → $150B)
- Net cash $38B + non-core stakes (Ant, ~33%) ~$30B haircut
- Per-ADS SOTP "bear" → ~$110-130

**But the bear floor must include political-tail scenarios that are NOT a simple haircut:**

| Tail event | Floor estimate | Recovery? |
|---|---|---|
| HFCAA forced delisting (PCAOB inspection lapse 2 consecutive years) | ADR → ~50-60% discount during forced conversion to HK 9988 shares; many US institutional holders **mandate-prohibited** from holding HK shares → forced selling | Yes, eventually, if conversion mechanics hold (BABA is dual-primary listed since 2022) |
| US Treasury OFAC/Executive Order restricting US capital into China tech | ADR holders required to divest, no buyer of last resort | Total loss of US-listed value possible (60-90% drawdown) |
| PRC capital control / VIE structure invalidation | Dividend remittance interrupted, ownership claims clouded | Permanent impairment risk |
| Taiwan kinetic event | All China assets re-rated to distressed multiples | Multi-year recovery if any |

**用 bear 下限不用当前现金:** Bear floor in a non-tail world = ~$95-105; in a 20% political-tail world expected floor = $60-80; in a 5% kinetic-tail world = sub-$40. **The downside is multi-modal and the modes are governed by external state-actor decisions I cannot predict.**

### 1.3 跌 50% 敢加？

50% drawdown from $121.75 = **$61**. At $61:
- If thesis intact and drawdown is mispricing → 0.7x P/B, 8x fwd EPS, 4-5x EV/EBITDA = generational value
- If drawdown caused by HFCAA delisting trigger / OFAC action / VIE invalidation → ADR may not exist or may be illiquid stub
- **I cannot, ex-ante, tell which regime I'm in at $61.** That's the test failing.

Regime-change vs mispricing is precisely the boundary I cannot adjudicate without inside-track political read. Buffett analogy: this is not a "value stock at half off" — this is a "value stock at half off OR a country-risk derivative trading away from par." The two have different add behaviors.

**原则1 verdict: FAIL.** This stays ETF-only (KWEB/MCHI/EMXC exposure as needed); no concentrated single-stock position.

## 2. 原则2 — 四维度 + 壁垒 (if hypothetically we passed P1)

Documenting for补习 purposes only — does not unlock a position.

### 2.1 壁垒 by line of business

| Line | Moat | Durability 2-3 yr | Direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| TTG (Taobao/Tmall CMR) | Two-sided network + merchant tooling + AI feed | Real but eroded by PDD/Douyin since 2020-2024 | **Stabilizing** (CMR +8% LFL Q4 FY26 vs flat-to-down prior) |
| Cloud (CIG IaaS/PaaS) | Hyperscaler infra + 36% PRC share + Aliyun region density (92 AZ / 29 regions) | Real | **Strengthening** (40% ext rev growth, accelerating from 18% → 26% → 34% → 40%) |
| MaaS (Qwen/Bailian) | Open-source distribution flywheel (1B+ HF downloads) + cost leadership via T-Head chips | Real but contested | **Building** — target ARR RMB 10B Jun-Q, RMB 30B by year-end |
| T-Head chips | Sovereign-chip narrative + 60% capacity to external customers | Defensible **only because** US export controls protect against NVIDIA in PRC | **Strong tailwind from policy**, fragile to policy reversal |
| Quick commerce | Scale + Ele.me last-mile + Taobao traffic | Weak | **Cash burn**, durability TBD |
| AIDC International | Brand presence + cross-border logistics via Cainiao | Modest | Negative EBITA, narrowing |

**Key insight:** The Cloud + MaaS + T-Head bundle is the *only* line with a strengthening moat — and that moat is structurally subsidized by US export controls on NVIDIA into China. If those controls relax (low-probability but not zero), the moat compresses materially.

### 2.2 四维度 grading

| Dimension | Pass? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ① 高增长 | **Partial** | Group +11% YoY Q4 FY26; Cloud +40%; CMR +8%; but FY26 operating income $7.3B vs $19.4B prior — growth-via-margin-destruction |
| ② 极致稳定+避险 | **NO** | Net income volatility extreme (FY26 EBITA collapse), political-tail risk uncapped |
| ③ 股东回报 | **Marginal pass** | Dividend +50% ($1.05 special), but **buybacks cut from $12B → $1.1B** to fund AI capex. Per CLAUDE.md "不回购的 floor 打折" |
| ④ 想象力 | **Yes** | $100B 5-yr cloud+AI external rev target; Qwen consumer flywheel; MaaS ARR ramp |

**Attack/defense grouping:** 进攻型 (满足 ① 或 ④) — yes if you grant cloud-as-growth + AI想象力. **Not 防御型** — fails ② badly.

## 3. 原则3 — 胜率 × 赔率 (hypothetical, since P1 blocks)

### 3.1 Win-rate decomposition

Decompose because political tail is independent of business thesis:

**Business win-rate** P(Cloud sustains ≥30% growth 2 yrs AND CMR stays ≥5% YoY AND quick commerce stops bleeding cash by FY28) ≈ **45-55%**
- Cloud trajectory robust (~70%); CMR stabilization more fragile (~55%); quick commerce profitability is consensus-priced as "happens" but red-queen race makes me skeptical (~40%).

**Political tail probability** P(no HFCAA/OFAC/VIE/Taiwan major shock through end-2028) ≈ **65-75%**
- Trump 2 administration, US-China rivalry escalating. Base rate of "no major shock in 2-yr window" has fallen materially vs 2024. PCAOB inspection currently functioning; BABA not on Commission-Identified Issuer list **for now**.

**Joint thesis win-rate** = ~0.50 × ~0.70 = **~35%**

### 3.2 Payoff (2-year, from $121.75)

| Scenario | Target | Multiple of bear | Prob (after P1 fail, illustrative only) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bull (cloud 50%+ growth, AI ARR > RMB 50B, no political event) | $260 (2.1x) | 4.3x bear | 20% |
| Base (cloud 30-40%, AI ramp on track, no political event) | $185 (1.5x) | 3.1x bear | 35% |
| Bear (capex overshoot, quick-commerce drag, CMR fades, no political event) | $85 | 1.4x bear | 25% |
| Tail (HFCAA forced conversion / OFAC / VIE) | $40-60 | 0.7x bear | 20% |

**E[Return] math:** 0.20 × 2.13 + 0.35 × 1.52 + 0.25 × 0.70 + 0.20 × 0.41 = 0.43 + 0.53 + 0.17 + 0.08 = **~1.21x in 2 years** (~10% IRR)
**Payoff (Base ÷ Tail-Bear):** $185 / $40 = ~4.6x **before** prob-weighting; **after** prob-weighting, expected loss in 20% of worlds dominates expected gain in 55% of worlds.

### 3.3 进攻型 2x test (CLAUDE.md hard gate)

> 进攻型大仓 = 下限明确 **且** 2 年 base case ≥ 2x（都要）

- 下限明确？ **NO.** Political-tail floor is multi-modal and external-actor-controlled.
- 2 年 base case ≥ 2x？ **NO.** Base $185 / $121.75 = **1.52x**, not 2x.

**Both legs fail.** Even ignoring 原则1, position would route to: 进攻但够不到 2x → **buy QQQ instead** (or in this case, KWEB if you want China exposure).

### 3.4 低胜率高赔率 → small position?

| Test | Result |
|---|---|
| Win-rate low? | 35% — yes |
| Payoff high (>3x)? | Tail-bear payoff is 4.6x (Base $185 / Bear $40), so yes |
| Floor explicable? | **NO** — political tail makes floor non-decomposable |

CLAUDE.md gate on 低胜率高赔率: "封顶 5%" — but the framework also says **"永远画得出 2x 但下限是 0/1 的标的→只能小仓"**. That's the closest pattern match here. Even so, this presumes 原则1 passed, which it didn't.
